Spaghetti Carbonara

25 min
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 3 large eggs
  • 75g Pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Preparation

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until it's crispy.
  3. Whisk together eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve with a sprinkle of black pepper and extra cheese.
