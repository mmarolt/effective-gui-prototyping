Classic Tomato Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 400g ripe tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, chop the tomatoes and garlic.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Drain spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  6. Garnish with basil and serve hot.
